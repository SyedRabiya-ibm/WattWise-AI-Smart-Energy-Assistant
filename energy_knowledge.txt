Energy Saving Knowledge Base

Electricity Saving:
Switch off unused appliances. Use LED bulbs. Use energy-efficient appliances. Avoid standby power consumption.

Air Conditioner:
Keep AC temperature between 24 and 26 degree Celsius. Clean filters regularly. Close doors and windows while using AC.

Solar Energy:
Solar panels reduce electricity bills and provide renewable clean energy.

LED Lighting:
LED bulbs consume less electricity and have longer life compared to traditional bulbs.

Refrigerator:
Avoid frequent door opening. Maintain proper temperature. Keep ventilation space around refrigerator.

Smart Home:
Smart meters and IoT devices help monitor and optimize electricity usage.
